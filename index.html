Football In South Africa

With an estimated 4 billion fans worldwide, football is the most popular sport globally.
Although football is very popular in South Africa, English Premier League Clubs tend to get more support than local ones.
However, the national team (Bafana Bafana) are very well supported and most of their matches sell out.

Football is played across a number of professional and amatuer leagues.

In South Africa, football has been played for around 150 years.
The first club in South Africa are called Savages FC (Pietermaritzburg).
